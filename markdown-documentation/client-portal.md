# Client Portal

The Client Portal is the main interface for clients to interact with the application. It provides a set of tools for clients to manage their pets, appointments, and personal information.

## Features

### Calendar View
Clients can view all their upcoming and past appointments on a full-sized calendar. This provides a clear and intuitive overview of their schedule.

### Appointment Management
Clients have full control over their appointments. They can:
*   Request new appointments for their pets.
*   View a list of all their past and upcoming appointments with their current status (Pending, Scheduled, Completed, Denied).
*   Receive notifications when the status of their appointment changes.

### Pet Management
Clients can manage profiles for each of their pets. This includes:
*   Adding new pets with details like name, breed, and age.
*   Uploading a profile photo for each pet.
*   Viewing a list of all their registered pets.
*   Clicking on a pet to view their individual profile page.

### Profile Management
Clients can view and manage their own profile information, including:
*   Viewing their contact details.
*   Uploading a personal profile photo.
*   Viewing a list of all their pets.
